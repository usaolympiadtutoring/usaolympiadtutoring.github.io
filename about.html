Our Approach

Olympiad chemistry is about much more than memorising facts or formulas. Our goal is to help students develop a deep understanding of chemistry and the problem-solving skills needed to tackle unfamiliar challenges with confidence. Sessions are tailored to each student's strengths, weaknesses, and goals, whether they are aiming for USNCO qualification, study camp, or IChO selection.

Conceptual Depth

Strong problem-solving starts with strong foundations. We help students understand the underlying principles behind chemical phenomena so they can apply their knowledge flexibly in unfamiliar situations.

Problem-Solving Practice

Olympiad success requires more than content knowledge. Through carefully selected problems and guided discussion, students develop intuition, recognize patterns, and learn effective strategies for tackling challenging questions.

Personalized Pacing

Every student begins at a different stage. Lessons are tailored to each student's strengths, weaknesses, and goals, ensuring steady progress and the right level of challenge throughout their preparation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What topics do you cover?

We cover all areas of Olympiad chemistry, including physical, organic, inorganic, and analytical chemistry. Depending on your goals, sessions can focus on specific topics, comprehensive USNCO preparation, IChO-level material, or general problem-solving strategies.

How long is each session?

Most sessions are 60 minutes, though longer sessions can be arranged for intensive problem-solving, mock exams, or competition preparation. Scheduling is flexible and can be tailored to each student's needs.

What is your pricing?

Our current price is $160/hour.

How are lessons conducted?

All lessons take place through Zoom and are strictly one-on-one with active, engaging conversation between the tutor and the student. The tutor will engage and guide the student through new material.
